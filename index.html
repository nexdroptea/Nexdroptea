About Us

🌱 Nexdrop Tea

Nexdrop Tea is an emerging Indian tea brand committed to delivering high-quality, flavorful tea to every household. We combine traditional tea excellence with a modern business approach to create a brand that stands for taste, trust, and affordability.

Our journey started with a simple vision: "To make every cup of tea rich in taste, quality, and trust."

🎯 Our Mission

Our mission is to make premium quality tea accessible and affordable across India, while creating strong business opportunities for retailers and distributors.

👁️ Our Vision

We aim to build Nexdrop Tea into a brand that becomes a trusted choice in every household, establishes a strong presence in both retail and wholesale markets, and expands from Bihar and Uttar Pradesh to all over India.

🏭 What We Do

  • Supply premium quality tea (B2B & B2C)
  • Offer high-margin retail products like 3KG jar packs
  • Build and support distributor & retailer networks
  • Deliver value-for-money tea with consistent quality

💪 Our Strengths

✔ Superior taste and aroma
✔ Affordable pricing with premium quality
✔ Strong supply and distribution network
✔ Better profit margins for retailers
✔ Backed by Nexdrop Solutions Pvt Ltd

🤝 Why Choose Nexdrop Tea?

In today's competitive market, Nexdrop Tea is not just a product—it's a business opportunity. We believe in growing together with our partners by offering quality products and reliable support.

Our Promise
